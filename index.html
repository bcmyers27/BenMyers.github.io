San Diego AirBnB Project

Data Cleaning In Excel

The dataset contains a lot of information, but a majority was useless for my purposes. I hid the columns that I would not be using for my analysis, to help my workflow. Next, I added filters to the spreadsheet so I could find discrepancies. Some listings were not within San Diego County and were removed. Others had the market listed wrongly or not at all and were corrected. A number of listings did not have zip codes associated with them and were removed. Listings without a nightly price or bedroom count were also removed as they did not fit into the scope of the project. I checked to make sure no listings were duplicated using conditional formatting and sorting.

Data Viz in Tableau

I created this dashboard to explore data on the AirBnB listings in San Diego. You can interact with the viz by clicking on the zip codes listed on the right side of the table. ## Plotting the dataset

Notes: looking for the correlation between body mass and flipper length between
the different species I plotted this graph

```{r}
ggplot(data=penguins)+
  geom_smooth(mapping=aes(x=flipper_length_mm,y=body_mass_g))+
  geom_point(mapping=aes(x=flipper_length_mm,y=body_mass_g,color=species))+
  labs(title="Palmer Penguins: Body Mass vs. Flipper Length",
       subtitle="Sample of Three Penguin Species",
       caption="Data collected by Dr.Kristen Gorman")+
  annotate("text", x=220,y=3500,label="The Gentoos are the largest",
           fontface="bold",size=4.5,angle=25)
```

## Conclusion

The scatter plot revealed that there is a correlation between body mass and
